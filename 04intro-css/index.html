Why Ski Utah?

Utah, renowned for its world-class ski resorts, offers an unparalleled winter sports experience with its abundant snowfall and stunning mountain landscapes. The state boasts famous destinations like Park City, Deer Valley, and Alta, providing diverse terrain for skiers of all levels. With its legendary powder snow, extensive trail systems, and picturesque surroundings, Utah stands as a premier destination for skiing enthusiasts seeking both thrilling slopes and breathtaking scenery.

We are lucky to have many ski resorts all over Utah. With 21 ski resorts, you are sure to find one that fits your needs!

Overview

  • Utah's ski resorts receive an average annual snowfall of around 500 inches, ensuring consistently excellent skiing conditions and earning the state a reputation for having some of the best powder snow in the world.
  • The state is home to the "Greatest Snow on Earth," a term trademarked by Utah to highlight the light, fluffy nature of its snow, making it ideal for skiing and attracting winter sports enthusiasts from around the globe.
